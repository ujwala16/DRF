Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be in college to attend?

Yes, this event is exclusively for college students. You must present
your active college ID to get in, even if you RSVP as well. If your school
doesn't give out ID's, please be prepared to pull up your current class
schedule to present along with an ID.

Can I bring a bag in?

For expedited access, leave bags at home. We have SWAG Bags for
you to carry all of your free stuff. Fenway Park security has final say on
whether or not a bag or person will be admitted.

Besides the T, how can I get there?

If you don’t want to take the T, you can reach Fenway Park using
rideshare services. There are also many nearby parking garages, and
some limited street parking.

Why should I RSVP?

RSVPing will get the latest event info, and to stay informed about
giveaways and prizes. We give away a lot of prizes (tickets, gift cards,
free products) randomly to our RSVP's! You'll also get access to the
VIP line, which will help you bypass the general admission line, getting
you in faster.

If I do RSVP, do I need to print out my ticket?

You can either print out your ticket or pull it up on your phone. Don't
forget that you'll still need an active college ID, even with an Eventbrite
ticket.

Do I have to wear a mask/be vaccinated?

As of April 2022, face masks and proof of vaccination are not required
to enter the park. Please note that this could change depending on the
local statistics and guidleines put in place by Fenway Park, the City of
Boston or the state of Massachusetts.
